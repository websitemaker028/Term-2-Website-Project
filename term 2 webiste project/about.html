About

Classical music is music that was composed in the late 18th and early 19th century. The Classical era was from about 1730 to 1820. This period of time in music was notable for the development of the sonata by outstanding composers such as Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart. Many well-known instrumental pieces were composed during the Classical era. These pieces include Eine Kleine Nachtmusik by Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart, Fur Elise by Ludwig Van Beethoven, and the Four Seasons by Antonio Vivaldi.
